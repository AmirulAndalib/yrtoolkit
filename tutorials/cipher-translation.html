Let's make an app that uses ciphers to create an Escape the Room-style game. We'll add two levels. The first will be to use Caesar's cipher to decode an answer. The second will be to translate text from Russian to English. In order to move from the first level to the second level, you must add the correct blocks in order to get the answer!

Connect using the companion app so you can test this on your mobile device. If you don't know how to connect, click here.

Let's some blocks to the app to make it more interesting.

Click on the Blocks button on the top right of the screen.

In Blocks, click decrypt.

Drag out the "when decrypt.Click" block.

Click on decrypted.

Drag out and connect "set decrypted.Text".

Click Procedures.

Drag out and connect the "call decrypt" prodecure.

Click Math.

Drag out and connect the "0" block and connect it to the shift socket. Change the 0 to 3.

Click on encrypted.

Drag out the "encrypted.Text" block and connect it to the text socket.

Test the app out in your companion device. Can you get to the next page through the app?

Let's add some features to the translation screen.

If you're not already there, go to Screen2.

Click on translate in the Blocks panel.

Drag out the "when translate.Click" block.

Click on YandexTranslate1.

Drag out the "call YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation" block and connect it.

Click Text.

Drag out the "" block and connect it to the languageToTranslateTo socket. Change the text to "ru-en" to tell the block what language you're translating from and to. You can find all of the language codes here.

Click answer.

Connect the "answer.Text" block to the textToTranslate socket. This tells the block where to get the text that it will translate.

Next, click on YandexTranslate1 again.

Drag out the "when YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation" block.

Click translation.

Add the "set translation.Text to" block.

Hover over the red translation variable and click on "get translation".

Add the "get translation" block to the last socket. This sets the text of this label to the translation that we got earlier.

Try it out on your companion device. Does the translation work? Were you able to get the right answer?
