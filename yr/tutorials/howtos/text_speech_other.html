1. Add TextToSpeech Component

Switch to the Designer.

In the Designer, click on the Media drawer and drag and drop a TextToSpeech component onto the Viewer. It will show up in the non-visible components section.

2. Change Country and Language Properties

Go to the Properties panel and find Country. We'll make our TextToSpeech component speak in Spanish. Go to the "Country" dropdown and choose "MCO" for Mexico.

Change "Language" to "es" for Spanish.

3. Make the App Speak

Switch to the Blocks Editor.

Click on TextToSpeech1 and add call TextToSpeech1.Speak into the when YandexTranslate1.GotTranslation block.

Hover over the orange translation button and click on get translation. Drag it to the "message" socket of call TextToSpeech1.Speak.

Test out your app! When the Translate button is pressed, it should translate from English to Spanish and speak the Spanish. Note that speaking text in different languages does not work on all mobile devices.
