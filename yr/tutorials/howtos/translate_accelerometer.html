1. Add an AccelerometerSensor

Switch to the Designer.

Open the Sensors drawer and drag an AccelerometerSensor to the Viewer.

2. Add when AccelerometerSensor1.Shaking

Click Blocks to switch to the Blocks Editor.

Select AccelerometerSensor1 in the Palette window and drag out a when AccelerometerSensor1.Shaking block.

3. Duplicate blocks

Right-click on call YandexTranslate1.RequestTranslation and click "Duplicate". Drag your duplicate blocks into when AccelerometerSensor1.Shaking.

4. Test it out!

You should be able to translate your text by shaking or clicking the "Translate" button. Note that if your mobile device does not have an Accelerometer Sensor, this will not work.
