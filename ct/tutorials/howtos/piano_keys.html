1. Change Key Colors

Switch to the Designer.

Changing the key color is really easy! Change the BackgroundColor of all regular buttons to white, and the BackgroundColor of all Sharp buttons to a black. You will have to change the TextColor for the black buttons to white so they appear on the black background.

2. Update Layout

To make the white buttons show up, change HorizontalArrangement1’s BackgroundColor to a light grey (or some other color).

And change the AlignVertical property to “Top: 1” for HorizontalArrangement1.

You could make the sharp note Buttons not quite as tall as the regular notes. 40% is a good option, but you can try different values to see what you like.

3. Test it out

Now see what your layout looks like on your mobile device, or in the emulator. It should look something like the image below, but you can always change the Button properties to make it look the way you like it.
